    Upon acceptance of employment with Hexaware, you will receive a packet of new hire forms via DocuSign. In addition to those forms, we also request that you take the following action:

    • State tax form

    • Participant Enrollment 401(K) form

    • Beneficiary Designation 401(K) form

    • Click on “state tax form” below and download, complete and return your applicable state tax form to NAonboarding@hexaware.com within 3 days of joining.
    • Review the direct deposit instruction guide and follow the instructions to input your direct deposit details as soon as you have access to StationH (Hexaware’s Intranet site).
    • After 30 days of employment, you will be eligible to enroll in Hexaware’s 401k plan.  You may enroll any time once the 30-day waiting period has elapsed.  When you are ready to enroll, please complete the 401k enrollment and beneficiary forms below and send them to hemantkumarg@hexaware.com for processing.  Specific plan/investment details can be found in the “Other Documents” section of this portal.
